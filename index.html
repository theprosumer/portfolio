What I Like About Front End Devlelopment

Whether its Saas, e-commerce or everyday making a one-page parallax design for a job seeker, I enjoy projects that I know are going to be used everday. The front end can also contain fun things like animations and interactive elements. Or it can display marketing material, contain SEO information and display the work of UX designers. All things I am interested in. Seeing the front-end come together gives gratification to both my creative and business sides, and keeps me excited about seeing a project come together.

What I Like About the Back End

The back end excites a different part of my brain. Using structure and logic to make sure informaton is being processed and distributed correctly and quickly. It requires careful thought and planning and contains the meat of how an idea goes from conception to reality. The back end is important to making more complex websites, like community based sites, more friendly and useful. Handling the flow of information, compiling data, managing scalability, and updating the client and server with the right data at the right time to make for awesome user experiences.
